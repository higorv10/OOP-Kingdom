# 👑 Contributor Ranks

> *The kingdom remembers every stone laid.*
> As your contributions are merged, you ascend through the ranks of the realm.

---

## The Ranks

| Rank | Title | Requirement | What it means |
|:----:|-------|-------------|---------------|
| 🌱 | **Settler** | 1st merged PR | You've staked your claim. Your first building stands in the kingdom — permanently. |
| ⚒️ | **Craftsman** | 3 merged PRs | You're a recognised builder. The citizens rely on the structures you've created. |
| 🏛️ | **Architect** | 7 merged PRs | Your designs shape the skyline. You understand OOP at a level that shows in every class. |
| 👑 | **Royal Council** | 15 merged PRs | You sit among the kingdom's elite. Your word carries weight in matters of architecture and design. |

---

## How Ranks Are Tracked

Your rank is determined by the number of **merged PRs** you have — not submissions, not attempts. Only merged PRs count toward your rank.

Your rank is updated in `contributors.json` and displayed on the kingdom website once it launches.

---

Each rank isn't just a badge — it reflects a genuine growth in how you think about code.
